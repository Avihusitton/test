# Product Marketing Context: Gil's Therapy Practice

## The Brand & Creator
- **Name:** Gil
- **Profession:** Clinical Social Worker (MSW) and Emotional Therapist.
- **Expertise:** 1. Individual therapy for adults (focusing on trauma, emotional coping, and life transitions) using the "Derech" method.
  2. Child therapy and parent counseling (ages 0-8) using the "Medabrim Yahad" (Talking Together) method.

## Target Audience
- **Primary Segment 1 (Parenting & Children):** Mothers and parents of young children (ages 0-8) who are experiencing behavioral or emotional challenges.
- **Primary Segment 2 (Individual Therapy):** Women (ages 25-50) seeking a safe space to process trauma, manage emotional struggles, or improve their mental well-being.

## Tone of Voice
- **Style:** Professional, empathetic, and softly grounded ("מקצועי ורך, אבל לא מדי").
- **Vibe:** The tone must immediately convey deep understanding of the visitor's pain point or challenge. It should feel like a warm but highly professional clinical environment. 
- **Key Emotion:** Trust. The reader should feel: "She understands exactly why I am searching for help right now, and she has the clinical expertise to guide me."

## Primary Call to Action (CTA)
- 1. Click the dedicated WhatsApp button for a direct, discreet message.
- 2. Fill out a contact form to leave details for a callback.
- **CRITICAL CONSTRAINT:** Do NOT mention, suggest, or design any "online calendar booking" features. 

## Output Language Rule
- ALL generated marketing copy, website text, and UI elements MUST be written in precise, sensitive Hebrew.
